What is Food Waste?

Food waste happens when edible food is discarded instead of being consumed. This leads to environmental damage, economic loss, and hunger worldwide.

Simple Solutions

Plan Meals

Buy only what you need and avoid over-purchasing food.

Donate Excess

Share surplus food with those who need it.

Compost Waste

Turn food scraps into nutrient-rich compost.
